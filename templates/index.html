The Interactome
The Multiscale Interactome is a unique approach to understanding disease treatment. Here, you can explore how drugs interact with biological functions and physical protein-protein interactions, providing a comprehensive view of treatment mechanisms. Choose your parameters, dive in, and discover how drugs treat diseases even when they seem unrelated.
Try It Yourself
VIEW
View Original Research
1. From the 'Choose a Disease' dropdown menu, select the disease you wish to investigate.
2. Press 'Rank Interacting Drugs' to identify potential drug candidates that may interact with the selected disease.
3. From the 'Select a Drug' dropdown menu, pick a potential drug candidate.
4. With the 'Set Disease Impact Range' and 'Set Drug Impact Range' sliders, fine-tune the scope of the disease's and drug's influence on the body.
5. Finally, hit 'Create Interaction Map' to generate a visual representation of the potential interaction between the chosen disease and drug.
